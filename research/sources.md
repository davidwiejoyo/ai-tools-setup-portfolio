# Research Sources — LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy (B2B SaaS)

## Overview
This research focuses on analyzing high-performing LinkedIn creators in the B2B SaaS and adjacent space. The selected experts are practitioners who consistently demonstrate strong engagement, clear positioning, and repeatable content patterns.

The goal is not to collect generic advice, but to study creators whose content can be reverse-engineered into actionable strategies.
