## 1. Hook Patterns
- Uses **specific outcomes or promises**
  - e.g., “How I turned 1 idea into $2,000,000…”
- Also uses:
  - curiosity (“little-known technique…”)
  - strong claims (“90% of digital products fail…”)

**Insight:**
Hooks combine **specificity + curiosity**, making them both credible and engaging.
